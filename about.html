Meet Lexa

Lexa is an AI-powered chatbot created by Ohio Codespace. Designed to assist, chat, and simplify tasks, Lexa combines speed, intelligence, and user-friendly interaction in one place.

Built on Google’s Gemini 2.0 Flash, Lexa delivers fast and reliable responses. Whether you type, speak, or send images, Lexa adapts to your way of chatting.

✨ Key Features

  • Send Images as Prompts
  • Use Voice Messages as Prompts
  • Copy & Read Aloud Responses
  • Powered by Gemini 2.0 Flash
  • Privacy First: Chat stored locally
  • Image Generation not supported (yet)

This Privacy Policy explains how Lexa handles your data while you use the chatbot. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. Data Collection

- Only the text, images, or voice prompts you choose to send are processed.
- Lexa may access your microphone or image files, but only with your explicit permission.
- Images are never stored — only the one you select is processed as a prompt.

2. Chat History

Your chat history is stored only on your device for your convenience. You can clear it at any time. We do not store your chats on external servers.

3. Permissions

- Microphone: Required to send voice messages as prompts.
- Image Access: Required to send images as prompts.

4. Data Usage

The prompts you send (text, image, or voice) are processed by the Gemini 2.0 Flash model to generate responses. These prompts are not stored by Lexa itself.

5. Security

Lexa does not store personal information or share your data with third parties. Your data stays within your device unless you explicitly share it.

6. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. You will be informed within the app when significant changes occur.

7. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to Ohio Codespace.
